Professional, Easy-to-use e-Voting software

Votely is a professional software that allows organisations and institutions run polls with voters maintaining absolute anonymousity.

Votely is a free.

Votely is absolutely free for use. sign up, create a poll and send unique links to accredited voters.

Get started →

Create an Account

Sign up on votely with one simple step. Your privacy is of uttermost importance to us and that is why we do not require them.

Create a vote poll

Log in to your dashboard to create a new poll. Register and accredit candidates, set a date and time for the pooling event.

share with voters

Send an e-mail blast to accredited voters. This e-mail contains a one-time unique link to participate in the polling process.

Watch vote count

You can watch and monitor the secured voting process in real-time. Accredited voters would also see vote counts as they go in real time.
